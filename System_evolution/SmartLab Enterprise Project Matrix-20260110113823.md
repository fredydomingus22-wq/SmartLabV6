# Project Matrix: SmartLab Enterprise

## 1\. **Overview**
The SmartLab Enterprise project is designed to integrate advanced digital solutions into industrial processes, focusing on the food and beverage sector in Angola. The project aligns with ISO standards and aims to enhance production efficiency, quality assurance, and audit readiness.
* * *

## 2\. **Project Objectives**
*   **Quality-First Approach:** Ensure compliance with ISO 9001, 22000, 17025, HACCP, and BPF standards.
*   **Industrial Optimization:** Streamline processes in production, quality, and safety.
*   **Audit-Ready Systems:** Provide traceable and reliable documentation for internal and external audits.
*   **Scalable Architecture:** Support multitenancy and future growth.
* * *

## 3\. **Key Modules**

| **Module** | **Purpose** | **Standards** |
| ---| ---| --- |
| MES (Manufacturing Execution System) | Monitor and control production processes. | ISO 9001, HACCP |
| LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System) | Manage laboratory data and ensure test accuracy. | ISO 17025 |
| QMS (Quality Management System) | Oversee quality control and non-conformities. | ISO 9001, ISO 22000 |
| FSMS (Food Safety Management System) | Ensure food safety compliance and risk management. | ISO 22000, HACCP |
| BI & Reporting | Provide actionable insights and analytics. | ISO 9001 |
| IA & SPC (Artificial Intelligence & Statistical Process Control) | Automate data analysis and predictive maintenance. | ISO 9001, ISO 22000 |

* * *

## 4\. **Stakeholders**

| **Stakeholder** | **Role** | **Needs** |
| ---| ---| --- |
| Operators | Execute daily production tasks. | User-friendly interface, real-time data. |
| Quality Analysts | Ensure product meets quality standards. | Accurate test results, traceability. |
| Food Safety Officers | Manage food safety risks. | Compliance tools, risk assessments. |
| Process Engineers | Optimize production processes. | Data-driven insights, process monitoring. |
| Auditors | Verify compliance with standards. | Clear, traceable documentation. |
| Management | Oversee operations and strategic decisions. | High-level reports, ROI visibility. |

* * *

## 5\. **Integration Points**
*   **MES ↔ QMS:** Link production data with quality control for real-time adjustments.
*   **LIMS ↔ FSMS:** Ensure lab results directly inform food safety protocols.
*   **BI ↔ All Modules:** Centralize data for comprehensive reporting and analytics.
* * *

## 6\. **Risks and Mitigation**

| **Risk** | **Impact** | **Mitigation Strategy** |
| ---| ---| --- |
| Non-compliance with standards | Regulatory penalties | Regular audits, ISO-aligned development. |
| Data integrity issues | Loss of trust, inefficiency | Implement robust audit trails and backups. |
| User adoption challenges | Reduced system utility | Provide training and user-friendly interfaces. |
| Scalability limitations | System bottlenecks | Design for multitenancy and future growth. |

* * *

## 7\. **Success Metrics**
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Measured by reduced downtime and improved throughput.
*   **Compliance:** Zero non-conformities during audits.
*   **User Satisfaction:** Positive feedback from operators and analysts.
*   **ROI:** Tangible cost savings and productivity gains.
